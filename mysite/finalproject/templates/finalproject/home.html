Welcome to Base Camp.

About Us.


We're a group of outdoor enthusiasts who wanted
to create a platform where fellow adventurers
can share their stories and the outdoor community
can come together to do good and encourage
others to get outside! If we're not in the office,
we're probably on the mountain!

Our Goal.


We're a group of outdoor enthusiasts
hoping to encourage others to enjoy
outside. We believe creating a space
for fellow adventurers to share their stories
not only encourages others to get outside
but also strengthens the community we already
have. Our goal is get people outside and show
what our earth has to offer.
